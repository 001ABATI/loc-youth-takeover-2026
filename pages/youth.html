TOURNAMENT JOURNEY

TOURNAMENT RULES

Rules & Eligibility

Every participant is expected to uphold the values of Love of Christ Generation Church by displaying discipline, respect, teamwork and good sportsmanship throughout the tournament.

Eligibility

Only registered participants are eligible to take part in the tournament.

Team Allocation

Teams will be created and assigned by the Football Committee before the tournament begins.

Sportsmanship

Respect your teammates, opponents, referees and Church Elders at all times.

Fair Play

Fighting, abusive language, cheating or misconduct will not be tolerated.

Punctuality

Arrive before your scheduled match time. Late arrival may affect participation.

Selection Decision

The final decision on player selection rests with the Church Elders and Football Committee.

Important Notice

Completing this registration does NOT automatically make you a member of the official LOC Football Team. Every registered participant will first compete in the tournament. Selection into the official team will be based on performance, discipline, teamwork and commitment throughout the competition.
